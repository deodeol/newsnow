Question and Answer about Jotamastic 80 Application Guide

Q: Can I apply Jotamastic 80 directly to rusty surfaces?

A: No, it is essential to remove any rust or loose particles before applying Jotamastic 80. Proper surface preparation is key to achieving a long-lasting and effective coating.

Q: Can I thin Jotamastic 80 for easier application?

A: No, it is not recommended to thin Jotamastic 80. The coating is designed to be applied as is, without any dilution.

Q: How long should I wait between coats of Jotamastic 80?

A: The recommended waiting time between coats of Jotamastic 80 is typically 4-24 hours, depending on the temperature and humidity. It is crucial to follow the manufacturer's instructions for the best results.

Q: Can Jotamastic 80 be applied over existing coatings?

A: Yes, Jotamastic 80 can be applied over existing coatings, as long as they are in good condition. However, it is essential to ensure proper adhesion by following the recommended surface preparation guidelines.
