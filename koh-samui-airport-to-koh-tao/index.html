Getting from Koh Samui Airport to Koh Tao

When it comes to getting from Koh Samui Airport to Koh Tao, there are several transportation options to choose from. The most popular and convenient way to travel is by taking a ferry from the airport to the island. The ferry ride takes approximately 2-3 hours, depending on the weather conditions and the speed of the ferry. Alternatively, you can also take a private taxi or shared minivan from the airport to the pier, and then catch a ferry to Koh Tao. This option is faster but more expensive than taking the ferry directly from the airport.
