History and Myth of Independence Day 2021 Speech

The tradition of delivering a speech on Independence Day dates back to August 15, 1947, when India gained its freedom from British rule. The first Prime Minister of India, Jawaharlal Nehru, delivered a historic speech from the Red Fort in Delhi, which set the tone for future Independence Day speeches. Since then, it has become a tradition for the Prime Minister to address the nation on this day.

Over the years, Independence Day speeches have become a symbol of hope, progress, and unity. They have played a crucial role in shaping the nation and inspiring generations of Indians. These speeches have become a part of our collective memory and serve as a reminder of the struggles and sacrifices made by our freedom fighters.
