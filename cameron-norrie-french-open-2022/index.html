What is the French Open and its Target?

The French Open, also known as Roland Garros, is one of the four Grand Slam tennis tournaments held every year. It takes place on the clay courts of the Stade Roland Garros in Paris, France. The target of the French Open is to bring together the top tennis players from around the world to compete for the prestigious title. It is known for its unique playing surface and challenging conditions, which test the players' physical endurance and mental fortitude.
