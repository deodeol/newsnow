What is a threaded metal fastener?

A threaded metal fastener refers to a type of hardware that is used to join two or more objects together. It consists of a screw-like shaft with a threaded end and a head that allows for easy tightening and loosening. Threaded metal fasteners are commonly used in construction, manufacturing, and DIY projects, and they come in various sizes and shapes to suit different applications.
