How to Make Coney Island Hot Dog Chili Recipe

Making Coney Island hot dog chili is easier than you might think. Start by browning ground beef in a skillet over medium heat. Add finely chopped onions and garlic, cooking until the beef is fully cooked and the onions are translucent. Drain any excess fat. Next, add tomato sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and a blend of spices such as chili powder, cumin, paprika, and garlic powder. Stir well to combine, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. The chili should thicken and the flavors should meld together. Serve the chili on top of grilled hot dogs, along with your favorite toppings such as onions, mustard, and cheese. Enjoy the delicious taste of Coney Island in your own home!
