Tennis World Rankings Current: Explained

Tennis world rankings are a system that determines the standing of professional tennis players in relation to one another. The more successful a player is in these tournaments, the higher their ranking will be. The rankings are updated on a weekly basis, allowing fans and players alike to keep track of the current top players in the world.

The concept of tennis world rankings was first introduced in the 1970s by the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP) and the Women's Tennis Association (WTA).
