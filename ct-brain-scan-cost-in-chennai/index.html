Question and Answer

Q: How long does a CT brain scan take?
A: A CT brain scan typically takes around 10 to 15 minutes, depending on the complexity of the scan.

Q: Are CT brain scans covered by insurance?
A: It depends on your insurance coverage. Some insurance plans may cover the cost of a CT brain scan, while others may require prior authorization or have specific criteria for coverage.

Q: Is a CT brain scan painful?
A: No, a CT brain scan is a painless procedure. You will be asked to lie still on a table while the scan is being performed.

Q: Are there any risks associated with a CT brain scan?
A: CT brain scans are generally considered safe. However, they do involve exposure to ionizing radiation, so it is important to discuss the risks and benefits with your healthcare provider.
