What is Too Good To Go App?

The Too Good To Go app is a platform that connects users with local restaurants and grocery stores that have excess food. Instead of letting this food go to waste, these businesses offer it at a discounted price through the app. Users can browse available food options, place an order, and then pick up their meal during a designated time window. It's a win-win situation for both users and businesses, as it reduces food waste and allows users to enjoy affordable meals.

History and Myth of Too Good To Go App

The Too Good To Go app was founded in 2015 by a group of friends in Denmark who were passionate about reducing food waste. They recognized that a significant amount of food was being thrown away by restaurants and grocery stores, even though it was still perfectly edible. To address this issue, they created the app as a way to connect these businesses with users who wanted to purchase the excess food. The app quickly gained popularity and expanded to other countries, becoming a global movement to combat food waste.
