Target of the Israeli Army

History and Myth of the Israeli Army

The Israeli army traces its roots back to the early days of the state of Israel. After the establishment of Israel in 1948, the country faced immediate threats from neighboring Arab nations. The IDF quickly mobilized to defend the newly formed state, and through a series of conflicts, including the War of Independence and the Six-Day War, the Israeli army established its reputation as a formidable force. Over the years, the Israeli army has become the subject of many myths and legends. From the daring rescue at Entebbe to the legendary intelligence operations, the IDF has captured the imagination of people around the world. These stories, along with the army's commitment to protecting its people, have created a sense of pride and admiration for the Israeli army.
