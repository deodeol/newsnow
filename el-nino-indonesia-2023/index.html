Understanding El Nino Indonesia 2023 and its Impacts

El Nino Indonesia 2023 is a climate phenomenon that is part of the El Nino Southern Oscillation (ENSO) cycle. This can result in reduced rainfall in Indonesia, leading to droughts, water shortages, and increased risk of wildfires.
