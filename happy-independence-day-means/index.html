History and Myth of Happy Independence Day

The history of Independence Day dates back to the late 18th century when many countries were fighting for their independence from colonial powers. In the case of the United States, Independence Day commemorates the signing of the Declaration of Independence on July 4, 1776, which declared the United States as a free and independent nation. The myth of Independence Day is often associated with fireworks, parades, and barbecues, which have become traditional ways to celebrate this day.
