So, what exactly is ombre adalah? Ombre adalah is a French term that translates to "shadow" or "shade" in English. It refers to the gradual blending of one color to another, typically from dark to light. This technique involves applying color to the mid-lengths and ends of the hair, while keeping the roots darker for a natural-looking effect. The result is a beautiful and seamless transition of color that adds depth and dimension to any hairstyle.
