Understanding El Nino in More Detail

To understand El Nino meaning in Malayalam and its implications, it is important to delve into the science behind this weather phenomenon. El Nino occurs when there is a shift in atmospheric pressure, causing warm surface waters to move eastward from the western Pacific Ocean. This disrupts normal weather patterns, leading to changes in rainfall, temperature, and wind patterns around the world.
