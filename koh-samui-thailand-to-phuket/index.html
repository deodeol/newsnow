Question and Answer

Q: How long does it take to travel from Koh Samui to Phuket?

A: The duration of the journey depends on the mode of transportation you choose. A flight takes around 1 hour, while a ferry ride can take between 4 to 6 hours.

Q: What is the best time to visit Koh Samui and Phuket?

A: The best time to visit Koh Samui and Phuket is during the dry season, which is from November to April. This is when you can enjoy the sunny weather and avoid the monsoon rains.

Q: Are there any direct flights from Koh Samui to Phuket?

A: Yes, there are direct flights available from Koh Samui to Phuket. Several airlines operate daily flights between the two destinations.

Q: What are the must-visit beaches in Koh Samui and Phuket?

A: Some of the must-visit beaches in Koh Samui include Chaweng Beach, Lamai Beach, and Bophut Beach. In Phuket, don't miss Patong Beach, Karon Beach, and Kata Beach.
