Independence Day Movie Series: History and Myth

The Independence Day movie series was first introduced to audiences in 1996 with the release of the original Independence Day film. Directed by Roland Emmerich, the movie became an instant success, grossing over $800 million worldwide. Its iconic scenes, such as the destruction of famous landmarks like the White House and the Empire State Building, have become ingrained in popular culture. The film's blend of thrilling action, impressive special effects, and memorable characters made it a box office hit and paved the way for the franchise's success.

The second installment in the series, Independence Day: Resurgence, was released in 2016. The movie continued the story of the original film, taking place twenty years after the events of the first invasion. The sequel expanded the mythology of the franchise and introduced new characters and alien threats, further immersing viewers in the world of the Independence Day movies.
