About "Coney Island Baby"

"Coney Island Baby" is a song by The Excellents, an American doo-wop group from Brooklyn, New York. The song was released in 1962 and became one of the group's biggest hits. It is known for its catchy melody, beautiful harmonies, and nostalgic lyrics about a summer romance at Coney Island. The song has since become a classic and is still beloved by fans of doo-wop music today.

A - D - E - A
