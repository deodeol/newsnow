Robert De Niro and Shahrukh Khan - The Legends

Both Robert De Niro and Shahrukh Khan have been in the industry for several decades and have left an indelible mark on the world of cinema. They have received critical acclaim and have won numerous awards for their performances. Some of his most memorable roles include Travis Bickle in "Taxi Driver" and Jake LaMotta in "Raging Bull". De Niro's commitment to his craft and his ability to immerse himself in his characters have made him one of the greatest actors of all time.

On the other hand, Shahrukh Khan is often referred to as the "King of Bollywood".
