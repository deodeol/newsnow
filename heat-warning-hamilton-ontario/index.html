What is a heat warning?

A heat warning is issued by Environment Canada when temperatures are expected to reach or exceed a certain threshold, combined with high humidity levels. This combination can pose a risk to your health, especially for vulnerable populations such as the elderly, young children, and those with pre-existing medical conditions. Heat warnings are important because they alert the public to the potential dangers of extreme heat and encourage individuals to take necessary precautions.
