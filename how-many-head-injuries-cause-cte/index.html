What is CTE?

CTE stands for chronic traumatic encephalopathy. It is a neurodegenerative brain disease that is believed to be caused by repetitive head trauma. CTE is characterized by the buildup of an abnormal protein called tau in the brain. This protein can cause significant damage to brain cells and lead to various cognitive, behavioral, and mood-related symptoms.
