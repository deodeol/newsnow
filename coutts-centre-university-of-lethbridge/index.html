What is Coutts Centre University of Lethbridge?

The Coutts Centre for Western Canadian Heritage is a unique facility that combines historical preservation with stunning natural surroundings. It is located on a 300-acre property that was generously donated by Dr. Jim and Virginia Coutts to the University of Lethbridge. The centre's mission is to promote an understanding and appreciation of the cultural and environmental heritage of the region.

The Coutts Centre offers a range of programming and events throughout the year, including art exhibits, workshops, and nature walks. It also provides a beautiful backdrop for weddings and special events, with its picturesque gardens and breathtaking views of the surrounding prairie landscape.
