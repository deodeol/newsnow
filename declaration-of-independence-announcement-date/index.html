Declaration of Independence Announcement Date: Explained

The Declaration of Independence announcement date, July 4, 1776, is a date that holds immense importance in American history. It was on this day that the Continental Congress adopted the Declaration of Independence, declaring the thirteen American colonies as independent states no longer under British rule. This momentous occasion marked the birth of the United States of America and the beginning of a new era of freedom and self-governance.
