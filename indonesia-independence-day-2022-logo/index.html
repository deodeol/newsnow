History and Myth of Indonesia Independence Day 2022 Logo

The history of Indonesia's struggle for independence is deeply intertwined with the mythology and legends of the country. By incorporating the Garuda in the logo, the Indonesian government pays homage to the rich cultural heritage of the nation and its people's resilience in the face of adversity.

The Hidden Secrets of Indonesia Independence Day 2022 Logo

While the Indonesia Independence Day 2022 logo may appear simple at first glance, it conceals several hidden secrets. This phrase is the national motto of Indonesia and reflects the country's commitment to embracing its multicultural identity.
