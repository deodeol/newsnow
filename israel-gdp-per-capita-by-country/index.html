Israel GDP per capita by country refers to the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) of Israel divided by its population. It is calculated by taking the total value of goods and services produced within a country's borders in a given year and dividing it by the country's population. This metric provides a measure of the average income per person in Israel and helps to compare the economic well-being of different countries.
