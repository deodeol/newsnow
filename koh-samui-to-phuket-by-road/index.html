If you're considering a road trip from Koh Samui to Phuket, you may have a few concerns. So, how do you get from Koh Samui to Phuket by road? The most common route is to take a ferry from Koh Samui to Surat Thani, and then drive from Surat Thani to Phuket. This journey takes approximately 6-7 hours, depending on the traffic and road conditions. Alternatively, you can take a ferry from Koh Samui to Donsak Pier, and then drive from Donsak Pier to Phuket, which takes about 4-5 hours.
