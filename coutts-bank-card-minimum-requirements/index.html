Coutts bank, a renowned private bank in the United Kingdom, has certain minimum requirements that applicants must meet in order to be eligible for a bank card. Coutts bank requires applicants to be residents of the United Kingdom. This is to ensure that the bank can provide its services within the country and comply with local regulations. Non-UK residents may be eligible for other banking services offered by Coutts, but may not qualify for a bank card.
