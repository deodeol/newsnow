How to achieve the perfect 4th of July nails dip

To achieve the perfect 4th of July nails dip, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by prepping your nails. Remove any old polish and trim and shape your nails as desired.
  2. Apply a base coat to protect your nails and help the powders adhere.
  3. Choose your desired colors for the design. Red, white, and blue are popular choices for a patriotic look.
  4. Dip your nails one at a time into the colored powders, tapping off any excess powder.
  5. Repeat the dipping process until you achieve your desired level of color and coverage.
  6. Finish with a top coat to seal in the design and add shine.
  7. Allow your nails to dry completely before applying any additional products or touching them.
