History and Myth of USA Independence Day Celebration Quotes

The history of Independence Day dates back to July 4, 1776, when the Continental Congress declared the thirteen American colonies to be free and independent states. This declaration, known as the Declaration of Independence, laid the foundation for the formation of the United States of America.

Over the years, Independence Day has become a day of celebration, marked by parades, fireworks, and gatherings. It is a day when Americans come together to honor their nation's birth and to reflect on the values that define their identity.
