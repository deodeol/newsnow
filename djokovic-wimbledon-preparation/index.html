Djokovic's physical training is focused on building strength, speed, and endurance. He follows a rigorous workout routine that includes weightlifting, cardio exercises, and agility drills. He also incorporates specific tennis drills to improve his technique and footwork.

Mental preparation is equally important for Djokovic. He practices mindfulness and meditation to stay focused and calm during matches. He also works with sports psychologists to develop mental strategies for dealing with pressure and maintaining a positive mindset.

In addition to physical and mental preparation, Djokovic also spends a significant amount of time analyzing his opponents and developing strategic game plans. He studies their playing styles, strengths, and weaknesses to come up with effective strategies to counter their moves.
