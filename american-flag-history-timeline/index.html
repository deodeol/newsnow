History and Myth of American Flag History Timeline

The history of the American flag is intertwined with myth and legend. One popular story is that Betsy Ross, a seamstress from Philadelphia, sewed the first American flag at the request of George Washington. However, it has become a part of the American folklore and adds to the mystique surrounding the American flag's history.

Hidden Secrets of American Flag History Timeline

Q: When was the first American flag design created?

A: The first American flag design, known as the "Grand Union Flag" or the "Continental Colors," was created in 1775.

Q: How often does the design of the American flag change?

A: The design of the American flag has changed 27 times throughout its history, with the most recent change occurring in 1960.
