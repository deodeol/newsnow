To explain maksud el nino adalah, we need to understand the underlying mechanisms of this phenomenon. El Nino is primarily driven by changes in atmospheric and oceanic circulation patterns. Normally, strong trade winds blow from east to west across the Pacific Ocean, pushing warm surface waters towards the western Pacific. This creates a temperature gradient, with warmer waters in the west and cooler waters in the east.

However, during an El Nino event, these trade winds weaken or even reverse, allowing warm surface waters to flow back towards the east. This leads to a massive redistribution of heat and moisture across the Pacific Ocean, which can disrupt normal weather patterns. The effects of El Nino can be felt globally, with impacts on rainfall, temperature, and storm activity.
