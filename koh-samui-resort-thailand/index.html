How to Get to Koh Samui Resort Thailand

To get to Koh Samui Resort Thailand, travelers can fly into Samui International Airport, which is serviced by both domestic and international flights. From the airport, it is a short drive to the resort. Alternatively, travelers can take a ferry from Surat Thani on the mainland to Koh Samui. The ferry journey takes approximately 1-2 hours, depending on the weather and sea conditions.
